Q&A

Q: WHAT IS PARALLAX PARADOX?

A: Parallax Paradox is a first person puzzle game set in a mysterious museum that seems oddly familiar to you. Perspectives shift in unusual ways. Is it a dream? Who knows. Escape and just maybe you can uncover the mysteries behind these exhibits.

Q: WHAT PLATFORM IS IT BUILT FOR?

A: Parallax Paradox is built for Windows, because that’s where our illusions behave themselves… mostly. Other platforms will just have to keep dreaming for now.

Q: WHERE CAN I DOWNLOAD IT?

A: See that big “Download Instructions” button on the home page? That’s not decorative. Click it, follow the steps, and the dream is yours.

Q: WHAT INSPIRED PARALLAX PARADOX?

A: We took the mind-warping puzzles of Superliminal & Antichamber, sprinkled in a dash of Portal, threw in some cel-shaded comic vibes, and asked, “What if your subconscious had an interior design budget and a questionable sense of humour?”

Q: WILL THERE BE A STORY BEYOND THE PUZZLES?

A: Of course. It’s hidden in the architecture, the recurring objects, and possibly behind that one suspicious couch in Level 4, Room 1 you never checked. Whether you figure it out or just start making wild guesses is entirely up to you.

Q: WHY A MUSEUM?

A: Because nothing says “surreal” like wandering through a gallery of impossible nonsense. Also, museums have big rooms — perfect for shoving in massive props, giant portals, and physics violations without breaking too many building codes.

Q: WHAT’S WITH THE ALARM CLOCKS?

A: They’re there to remind you that you should wake up at some point… but also to make you wonder if you’re running late for something important. Or unimportant. Or maybe they’re just in on the joke.

Q: IS THIS A HORROR GAME?

A: Only if you find optical illusions, shifting doors, and giant props terrifying.
